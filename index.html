How it started

A famous distillery approaches Dougal for beer to season their casks, leading to the ‘accidental discovery’ of our oak aged beer’s unique taste. Innis & Gunn is founded and the first bottles of our Original brew are sold. Initial trials take place with the Oakerator ® our one-of-a-kind invention for oak ageing beer. Off-sales get an online boost with our web shop’s launch, enabling beer buffs across the UK to order direct.
